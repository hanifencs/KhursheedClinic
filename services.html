GP OPD
GP OPD

The GP Outpatient Department offers general healthcare services for common health issues, including consultations, diagnosis, and treatment. It's the first place people go for basic medical care.

Gynae OPD
Gynae OPD

The Gynecology Outpatient Department provides care for women’s health, focusing on reproductive issues such as menstrual problems, infections, and hormonal concerns.

Management of Medical Emergencies
Management of Medical Emergencies

Emergency care for serious health issues like chest pain, infections, or breathing problems. Medical staff quickly assess and treat patients to stabilize their condition.

Trauma Management
Trauma Management

Care for injuries caused by accidents or falls, including treatment for wounds, fractures, and emergency stabilization to prevent further damage.

ECG
ECG

An Electrocardiogram (ECG) records the heart's electrical activity to help doctors diagnose heart problems like irregular rhythms or other cardiac conditions.

Ultrasound (Gynae and Obstetrics only)
Ultrasound (Gynae and Obstetrics only)

Ultrasound imaging is used for checking the health of a pregnancy and the fetus. It also helps diagnose gynecological conditions like cysts or fibroids.

Ante-Natal Services
Ante-Natal Services

Routine check-ups and screenings for pregnant women to ensure both the mother’s and baby’s health during pregnancy.

Management of Gynae Emergencies
Management of Gynae Emergencies

Emergency care for urgent female health issues such as heavy bleeding or pregnancy complications, providing immediate treatment and further care if needed.

Detention Facility
Detention Facility

A place where patients are kept temporarily for observation or isolation, usually until they are transferred to a hospital or discharged.

Minor Surgical Procedures
Minor Surgical Procedures

Simple, non-invasive surgeries like draining abscesses or stitching small wounds, usually done with local anesthesia and no overnight stay required.

Minor ENT Procedures
Minor ENT Procedures

Treatments for ear, nose, and throat problems, such as removing foreign objects from the ear or nose, or treating nasal bleeding.

Physiotherapy
Physiotherapy

Physical therapy helps people recover movement and strength through exercises and treatments, especially after injury, surgery, or long-term conditions.

Home Care Services
Home Care Services

Nursing or medical care provided at home for people recovering from surgery, managing chronic illness, or needing extra help with daily tasks.

Age Care Services
Age Care Services

Support and care for older adults, focusing on health monitoring, mobility assistance, and ensuring they live independently and comfortably.

Individual Support
Individual Support

Personalized help for individuals who need assistance with daily activities or emotional support, aimed at promoting independence and well-being.

Disability Services
Disability Services

Specialized care for people with disabilities, providing the support they need for daily living, improving their quality of life, and encouraging inclusion.

About Us

We are a dedicated team of healthcare professionals providing expert medical consultations and patient-focused care. Our mission is to improve health outcomes through quality service and compassionate support.
